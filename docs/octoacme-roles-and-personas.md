# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Key Interactions
- **With Technical Lead:** Collaborate on technical design decisions, seek guidance on architectural patterns, and escalate complex technical blockers
- **With Product Manager:** Clarify feature requirements, discuss feasibility and technical constraints, propose alternative solutions
- **With UX Designer:** Review design specifications for implementation feasibility, provide feedback on interaction patterns
- **With Business Analyst:** Validate data models and business logic, clarify edge cases and system behaviors
- **With QA/Testing:** Collaborate on test strategies, reproduce and fix bugs, ensure testability of code
- **With Scrum Master:** Report blockers, participate in ceremonies, provide input on process improvements

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Key Interactions
- **With Business Analyst:** Partner on requirements gathering, validate business rules and data needs, align on success metrics
- **With UX Designer:** Collaborate on user research, define user stories, ensure feature usability aligns with customer needs
- **With Technical Lead:** Discuss technical feasibility, negotiate trade-offs between scope and technical constraints
- **With Developers:** Clarify requirements, answer questions about acceptance criteria, prioritize bug fixes and technical debt
- **With Project Manager:** Align on roadmap priorities, communicate scope changes, coordinate stakeholder messaging
- **With Scrum Master:** Refine backlog items, participate in sprint planning and reviews

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Key Interactions
- **With Scrum Master:** Coordinate on team processes and ceremonies, align on sprint planning and retrospectives
- **With Product Manager:** Synchronize on priorities and roadmap changes, ensure scope alignment with business goals
- **With Technical Lead:** Monitor technical risks and dependencies, facilitate decision-making on architectural topics
- **With Business Analyst:** Ensure requirements are documented and traceable, coordinate on stakeholder communications
- **With UX Designer:** Integrate design milestones into project timeline, coordinate user testing and feedback sessions
- **With Developers:** Track progress and blockers, facilitate escalations, ensure resource availability

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business stakeholders and technical teams. They gather, analyze, and document business requirements, ensure data integrity, and validate that solutions meet business needs and compliance standards.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements from stakeholders
- Create process flows, data models, and business rules documentation
- Validate solutions against business requirements and acceptance criteria
- Analyze data and metrics to inform product decisions
- Facilitate requirements workshops and clarification sessions
- Ensure regulatory and compliance requirements are captured

### Goals
- Ensure clear, complete, and testable requirements
- Bridge communication gaps between business and technical teams
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business objectives
- Maintain traceability from requirements to implementation

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and user stories
- Data analysis reports and business case documentation
- Stakeholder interviews and workshops
- Acceptance criteria reviews with Product Manager and developers

### Key Interactions
- **With Product Manager:** Collaborate on requirements gathering, validate business cases, ensure alignment with product vision
- **With Developers:** Clarify business logic, answer questions about data flows, validate implementation against requirements
- **With UX Designer:** Ensure user workflows align with business processes, validate data capture and display requirements
- **With Project Manager:** Report on requirements completeness, track requirements changes, coordinate stakeholder reviews
- **With Technical Lead:** Discuss data architecture implications, ensure non-functional requirements are captured
- **With Scrum Master:** Refine user stories, ensure requirements are ready for sprint planning

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers create user-centered design solutions that are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with user needs. They conduct research, create wireframes and prototypes, and ensure consistency across the product experience.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define user flows and interaction patterns
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design practices
- Maintain design systems and component libraries
- Collaborate with developers during implementation

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and delightful user experiences
- Ensure accessibility and usability standards are met
- Reduce user friction and support task completion
- Maintain design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design specifications and style guides
- User research findings and personas
- Prototype reviews and usability test reports
- Design critiques and feedback sessions

### Key Interactions
- **With Product Manager:** Understand user needs and business goals, validate designs against product requirements
- **With Developers:** Provide design specifications, clarify interaction details, review implementation for design fidelity
- **With Business Analyst:** Align user workflows with business processes, ensure data requirements support user tasks
- **With Technical Lead:** Discuss technical constraints that impact design, explore feasible interaction patterns
- **With Project Manager:** Coordinate design milestones, communicate design dependencies and timeline impacts
- **With Scrum Master:** Participate in sprint planning, ensure design work is integrated into team cadence

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile practices and ceremonies, remove impediments, and coach the team to continuously improve. They protect the team's focus and foster a collaborative, high-performing culture.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove blockers and impediments to team progress
- Coach the team on agile practices and self-organization
- Shield the team from external interruptions
- Track team velocity and help with continuous improvement
- Foster psychological safety and open communication

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and velocity
- Ensure agile ceremonies are effective and time-efficient
- Remove impediments quickly to maintain team flow
- Build a culture of continuous improvement and collaboration

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- Retrospective facilitation and action item tracking
- Blocker escalations and resolution updates
- Team health metrics and improvement proposals

### Key Interactions
- **With Project Manager:** Coordinate on project timelines and resource needs, align on escalation paths
- **With Product Manager:** Facilitate backlog refinement, ensure user stories are ready for sprint planning
- **With Developers:** Remove technical and process blockers, facilitate collaboration and knowledge sharing
- **With Technical Lead:** Escalate technical impediments, coordinate on architectural discussions
- **With UX Designer:** Integrate design work into sprint planning, coordinate on design review sessions
- **With Business Analyst:** Ensure requirements are properly refined and ready for development

---

## Technical Lead

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide technical direction, architectural guidance, and mentorship to the development team. They make key technical decisions, ensure code quality and consistency, and bridge technical and business considerations.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical architecture and design patterns
- Review code and provide technical mentorship
- Make decisions on technology choices and technical standards
- Identify and manage technical risks and debt
- Ensure scalability, security, and performance considerations
- Collaborate on technical roadmap and feasibility assessments

### Goals
- Maintain high code quality and architectural consistency
- Build scalable, maintainable, and secure systems
- Grow team technical capabilities through mentorship
- Balance technical excellence with business delivery needs

### Typical Communication
- Technical design documents and architecture diagrams
- Code review comments and technical guidance
- Technical risk assessments and feasibility analyses
- Technical standards and best practices documentation

### Key Interactions
- **With Developers:** Provide technical mentorship, review designs and code, guide on best practices
- **With Product Manager:** Assess technical feasibility, negotiate scope based on technical constraints
- **With Project Manager:** Communicate technical risks and dependencies, provide estimates for technical work
- **With Business Analyst:** Review data models and technical requirements, ensure technical feasibility
- **With UX Designer:** Provide input on technical limitations, collaborate on feasible interaction patterns
- **With Scrum Master:** Identify and resolve technical blockers, participate in technical process improvements
